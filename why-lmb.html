Why LMB

Home / Why LMB

Among the many qualities of LMB products, the following stand out as a testament to why you too should be choosing LMB for yourself or your business

Manufactured with Care

In a world where quality is constantly degraded in the continuous push for mass-produced quantities, and where premium hand-manufactured products are often so underrated, LMB Computers shines a light of hope with its exceptional hand-assembled products aiming to exceed every buyer's highest expectation.

Unmatched Quality

We only use brand-new, high-quality components from reputable brands in the manufacturing of our products - unlike with our competitors, you can rest assured that you will never be handed knockoffs, used, or recycled goods.

Extended Warranty

We provide an extended 18-months (1 year and 6 months) limited warranty on all our products - because we are just so confident about the quality and reliability of our computers.

Energy Efficient

All the power supply units which we use in the manufacturing of our computers are at least 80 PLUS® Bronze compliant. The 80 PLUS® certification is intended to promote efficient use of energy in desktop computers. It comes in six different ratings, where the higher the rating, the more energy-efficient your computer is. The six levels start from Standard, and increase through Bronze, Silver, Gold, Platinum, up to Titanium. We indicate your computer's efficiency level in your personalised quote (or for our pre-built systems, in the product description).

Aftersales Service

Our aftersales service easily beats that of any other average South African business: our extensive support and dedicated customer services are committed to maintaining our homegrown quality and excellence.

Proudly Local

We are based in Cape Town, South Africa, where all the LMB magic happens.

Why Choose an LMB Desktop PC Tower

In a world where poor quality laptops dominate computer sales, here's why you need to opt for the smarter choice: a desktop (or "box") computer

Value for Money

We know: all of us South Africans are being constantly pressured with price increases left, right and centre, often forcing some of us to give up quality in favour of cost savings. However, you should not be forced to give up high quality and performance on a device that you will need and rely on so much every single day. All else being equal, desktop computers are significantly more affordable than any laptop you can find. You get a lot more "bang for your buck". How? Well, there is a lot of complex and detailed engineering going into making a device smaller and lighter as a laptop, and such engineering costs incurred by laptop manufacturers are passed onto you, the consumer. What happens then is that you end up paying nearly double for a laptop with similar specifications and power as an LMB desktop computer. Or, alternatively, you end up getting a much weaker, much slower and lower quality laptop for the same price as an LMB desktop computer. Rather get yourself a custom-built, tailor-made LMB desktop computer that significantly eclipses any laptop you would be able to pick up for the same price.

Unmatched Performance

We at LMB can manufacture desktop computers at considerably higher performance specifications than the best laptops available on the African market. The greater availability of space inside the computer "box" boasts much better air flow than any laptop, which in turn allows the internal components to push themselves to higher speeds and better performance without sacrificing the computer's longevity. This means that our LMB machines easily include notably better components at cooler temperatures. If your work and/or your hobbies require you to have the best of the best, an LMB desktop computer is undoubtedly the way to go.

Repairability

For those "oh no!" moments, when you drop your laptop, when you spill your cuppa coffee all over the keyboard, or when your child or pet jumps all over your laptop - it is always better to rather be the owner of an LMB desktop computer! Because of the extreme care and attention to detail which we implement in the manufacturing of our desktop computers, a typical 1 metre drop which usually forever damages your laptop beyond repair may, at worst, break the tempered glass side panel and slightly dent a corner of the LMB computer box - that is it! And spilling your favourite drink all over your laptop's keyboard means forever damaging all the internal components in your laptop - this is because laptop manufacturers solder all components together underneath the keyboard. On the other hand, spilling any liquid over a keyboard connected to a desktop computer may just mean you will have to replace your keyboard, but your LMB tower will not even blink!

Upgradeability

When a laptop begins to slow down, you find yourself in the typical "eish, one to dump and a new one to buy" situation. However, LMB's desktop computers are designed for upgradeability: each and every single component we use in the manufacturing process can be removed and replaced individually and as needed, which is not the case with most laptops built after 2009. Let us give you an example - say that, five or six years after purchasing one of LMB's most affordable low-end Home and Office desktop computers, you feel like it has become a bit slow and, after you bring it to us for an in-depth analysis, we notice that a specific component is struggling to cope with the latest technologies. While most IT companies - and certainly all laptop manufacturers - will tell you that your only alternative is to get rid of your device and buy a new one, LMB focuses on your real needs and tries to keep your expenses to a minimum without sacrificing performance and longevity: we will only recommend that you upgrade the component(s) that are slowing your computer's overall performance, and we promise that never will we ever milk you for cash by trying to push more upgrades than your computer requires to operate well.

Customisability

So-called "fixed peripherals" are perhaps one of the most significant drawbacks of any laptop. Fixed peripherals mean that when you buy a laptop, you are stuck with the same screen, keyboard, touchpad/trackpad, speakers and ports for the duration of its life. Buying laptop travel accessories, or an external mouse, keyboard or monitor completely nullifies the idea of portability which is often seen as attractive when purchasing a laptop. Hence, it might be cheaper and wiser to go for an LMB desktop computer instead - you are then free to add, remove, replace, upgrade or in any other way change any peripheral as you wish.

Isolate Work From the Rest

In today's ever-popular Work From Anywhere philosophy, an employee's need for fully unplugging from his or her job or work is completely ignored and totally disregarded by employers. So, don't rely on others to do it for you, and instead do it yourself: with a desktop computer dedicated to your home office, you will be able to shut it down when you are done, and get out of your work mindset (and out of your home office, too), enjoying the rest of the day as you most please.

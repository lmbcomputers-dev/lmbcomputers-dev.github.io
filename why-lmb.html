Why LMB

Home / Why LMB

Among the many qualities of LMB products, the following stand out as a testament to why you too should be choosing LMB for yourself or your business:

Manufactured with Care

In a world where quality is constantly degraded in the continuous push for mass-produced quantities, and where premium hand-manufactured products are often so underrated, LMB Computers shines a light of hope with its exceptional hand-assembled products aiming to exceed every buyer's highest expectation.

Unmatched Quality

We only use brand-new, high-quality components from reputable brands in the manufacturing of our products - unlike with our competitors, you can rest assured that you will never be handed knockoffs, used, or recycled goods.

Extended Warranty

We provide an extended 18-months (1 year and 6 months) limited warranty on all our products - because we are just so confident about the quality and reliability of our computers.

Energy Efficient

All the power supply units which we use in the manufacturing of our computers are at least 80 PLUS Bronze compliant. The 80 PLUS certification is intended to promote efficient use of energy in desktop computers. It comes in six different ratings, where the higher the rating, the more energy-efficient your computer is. The six levels start from Standard, and increase through Bronze, Silver, Gold, Platinum, up to Titanium. We indicate your computer's efficiency level in your personalised quote (or for our pre-built systems, in the product description).

Aftersales Service

Our aftersales service easily beats that of any other small-scale South African business: our extensive support pages and quick turn-around customer services are comparable to high-ranking, top-of-the-range global computer businesses, but do not sacrifice our homegrown quality and excellence to do so.

Proudly Local

We are based in Cape Town, South Africa, where all the LMB magic happens.
